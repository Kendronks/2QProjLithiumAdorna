Mechanics Terms

Terms (Alphabetical Order)

Meaning/Definition

Example Sentence

Beatmap

Refers to a playable song in the game that is "mapped" with circles to click to the beat

Hello, have you played this beatmap yet?

Mapper

People that make beatmaps

Are you a mapper? Can you map this song for me?

Player

People that set scores in the game

I'm an osu!std player

Sensitivity/sens

How sensitive your cursor reacts to your mouse movement

my sensitivity is pretty low.

Score/Play

One complete run of an entire beatmap, usually submitted to the server for recording

I set a pretty good score today.

Circle/Hitcircle

The object your cursor should be hitting on time

These circles are too weirdly spaced.

Approach Circle

A hollow circle closing in on the hitcircle, usually to telegraph the timing of the hit

The approach circles are too fast!

Slider

Another object that requires holding the keys to properly hit, has a head that must be hit properly, and a snake like body that must be followed with the cursor

These sliders are shaped weird

Spinner

The easiest object to properly hit, requires circular movement of your cursor to hit

 This spinner takes so long

Combo

The amount of consecutive objects you hit properly

I got 200 combo before missing

Miss

Failure to hit an object, either through misaiming or tapping too late/early

I missed on the last note!

Sliderbreak(sb)

misaiming the head of the slider

I sliderbroke on the first slider!

comboset

Easiest described as same color circles

 Why is there only one comboset on the map?

Accuracy

A percentage that describes the ratio of hitobjects to the amount of 300's, 100's and 50's

I have pretty high accuracy right now

Scorev1

osu!std scoring system, puts emphasis on combo

Scorev1 is so broken right now.

Hit Window

The amount of error given to hit a 300

The hit window on this map is so small!

300

A perfectly timed hit on an object based on the mapping

I'm getting nothing but 300's on this map

100

A slightly early/late timed hit on an object based on the mapping

How did I get a 100?

50

A moderately early/late timed hit on an object based on the mapping

How did I get a 50?

SS

Getting the highest possible score on the map in terms of scorev1

I SS'ed the map yesterday.

S

Over 90% 300's, less than 1% 50's and no misses

I S ranked the map today

A

Over 80% 300s and no misses, or over 90% 300s

I A ranked the map a few days ago.

Fail

losing all your HP on a beatmap

I just failed the map after 10 seconds.

Circle Size (CS)

A number (1-10) that dictates how small the circles are on a beatmap, the higher the number the smaller the circle

CS 4 is too small for me.

Approach Rate (AR)

A number (1-11) that dictates how fast the approach circle closes in on the hitcircle, the higher the faster

AR 10 is too fast for me

Overall Difficulty (OD)

A number (1-11) that dictates how tight the hit window is for a 300, 100, and 50, the higher the tighter

OD 9 is easy for me.

Health (HP)

A number (1-11) that dictates how much consecutive misses you can get before failing, the higher the less misses

HP 6 is impossible to pass without getting an S rank

Star Difficulty

The difficulty of the map represented by a number of stars

What's the star difficulty of the map? 5 Stars?

Skin

A modification to the default textures of the game

Look at this cool skin I found!

Mods

modifications to beatmap elements that make the game easier/harder

Mods are too hard for me right now.

Hard Rock (HR)

A mod that shrinks circle size and hit window

Hard rock is impossible to play with.

Double Time (DT)

A mod that applies a 1.5x multiplier to the bpm (also shrinks hit window)

Double time is too fast for me.

Hidden (HD)

A mod that removes approach circles

Hidden is a mod I always use.

Flashlight (FL)

A mod that limits your vision to a space around your cursor

Flashlight is such a rare mod to see people using.

Easy (EZ)

A mod that enlargens circle size and hit window

Easy isn't as easy as it sounds.

No Fail (NF)

A mod that disables failing a beatmap

I have to no fail the map, it's too hard.

Half Time (HT)

A mod that applies a 0.75x multiplier to the bpm (also enlargens hit window)

Why are you putting half time on?

Sudden Death (SD)

A mod that makes you fail after missing

Sudden death pressures me too much.

Perfect (PF)

A mod that makes you fail after not getting a 300

Perfect is really unforgiving.

Relax (RX)

A mod that automatically clicks for you

I have to put on relax, my fingers hurt.

Autopilot (AP)

A mod that automatically aims for you

I'm using autopilot, my wrist hurts.

Spun-Out (SO)

A mod that automatically completes spinners for you

I'm chilling out, so I'll use spun-out.

Auto (AT)

A mod that automatically plays the beatmap for you

I'll record a playthough of the map with auto.

Scorev2 (V2)

A mod that makes the score system different, putting more emphasis on accuracy

Do you wanna turn scorev2 on?

Performance Points (pp)

The main number used to rank players, acquired from setting scores on multiple different maps

How much pp do you have

Level

A metric of how long you have been playing the game

I'm level 100, what about you?

Aim-related Terms

Term

Definition/Meaning

Example Sentence

Aim

Skill to move cursor to circle

How's your aim, Is it rusty?

Jump Aim

Your proficiency in aiming circles that are spaced across the screen, with relatively long spaces between keystrokes

 My jump aim is rusty.

Stream Aim

your proficiency in aiming circles that are less spaced across the screen, with relatively short spaces between keystrokes

My stream aim is on point.

Spacing

the distance from one hitcircle to the next one you're supposed to hit

The circle's spacing is weird on this map

Follow Points

The lines that direct you on which circle to hit next

Follow points just confuse me sometimes.

Reading

Your ability to see which order the circles should be hit in

I can't read the map, the AR is too low!

Snap Aim

A fast, efficient motion in aiming one circle to the next

I can't snap to circles using my mouse.

Flow Aim

A slower, more relaxed motion in aiming one circle to the next

I just flow through all the circles, it's easier for me.

Angle

The angle at which combosets are spaced

What are those jump angles?

1-2's

A specific type of jump that only features 2 circles in the comboset

Get ready for the 1-2's

Triangles

A type of jump pattern that has 3 circles per comboset (shaped like a triangle)

Here come the triangles!

Squares

A type of jump pattern that has 4 circles per comboset (shaped like a square)

There's squares on after this section.

Stars

A type of jump pattern that usually has 5+ circles per comboset (shaped like a star/stars)

Star jumps are fun to hit.

Tapping-related Terms

Term

Definition/Meaning

Example Sentence

Tapping

Skill to use keyboard to time clicks to the rhythm of music

My tapping is weird today.

Singletapping

A tapping style that utilizes only one finger and one key

I can barely singletap 120 bpm.

Alternating

A tapping style that incorporates alternating both fingers and both keys

I can alternate almost every map.

Full-Alt

A tapping style that exclusively alternates both fingers and both keys

I use full-alt as my tapping style

Doubletapping

A tapping style that mashes both keys rapidly at the same time(usually because a section is too fast to tap to)

I doubletapped that entire section

Speed

Ability to consistently tap to a beat at an absurdly fast rate, or a high bpm.

I don't have speed today

Stamina

Ability to consistently tap over long periods of time

I don't have stamina today.

Unstable Rate (UR)

How close your hits are together (a measure of precision)

My UR is so high!

Doubles

A tapping pattern that involves 2 circles that are evenly spaced (rhythm-wise)

These doubles are so hard to hit.

Burst/Bursting

A tapping pattern that involves 3 circles that are evenly spaced (rhythm-wise)

These bursts are so fun to hit.

Stream/Streaming

Refers to a long, spaced set of 16-32 circles that are evenly spaced (rhythm-wise)

That stream is too hard for me.

Deathstream

Refers to a longer, spaced set of 32+ circles that are evenly spaced (rhythm-wise)

This deathstream is killing my fingers!

Rhythmic Patterns (Patterns)

Ways to organize timings of circles to follow a beat

What are these patterns even?

Pattern Changes

The changes in rhythmic patterns throughout a beatmap

Why are there so many pattern changes?

Reading

The ability to know when to tap a circle

I can't read when to tap the circle!

Musical Terms

Term

Definition/Meaning

Example Sentence

Rhythm

A pattern of sounds/beats

What's the rhythm of this song?

BPM/Beats Per Minute

A metric of the speed at which the song is played

What's the BPM of this song?

Feel

A persons grasp of a particular rhythm

I feel the groove differently from you.

Tempo Change

The change in tempo, or bpm of a particular song

Get ready for the tempo change.

Off-beats

Beats that are played weakly in comparison to the others

Make sure to hit the off-beats

Offset

Alters the timing of the notes, usually to fix audio errors/timing errors

Have you setup your offset yet?

triplets

3 evenly spaced notes in 1 beat

These triplets confuse me everytime.

eighth-notes

2 evenly spaced notes in 1 beat (1 beat is dictated by the bpm)

Eighth-notes are too slow for me.

sixteenth-notes

4 evenly spaced notes in 1 beat (1 beat is dictated by the bpm)

Sixteenth-notes are too fast for me.

Cultural Terms

Term

Definition/Meaning

Example Sentence

WYSI (When You See It)/727

A meme that surfaced in the community when the highest pp play was set at 727, on the same map twice, with different mod combinations (one HR, one HDHR)

727!, When you see it!

Tech

Generally a type of mapping style where there are a lot of objects on screen, and said objects are awkwardly placed, requiring a sort of technique to hit the patterns

Is this a tech map?

Finger Control

Ability to precisely control your keyboard fingers | Could also refer to a type of mapping style that requires precise finger control

My finger control is so good right now.

Cursor Shake

The cursor shaking when someone is setting an incredible score

Im shaking so much, look at the cursor shake!

3mod

Usually refers to a mod combination of hidden, double time, and hard rock, but any of the mods could be replaced with flashlight

I'm 3modding the map.

hdhr

Refers to the mod combination hidden and hard rock

I'm gonna HDHR the map.

hddt

Refers to the mod combination hidden and double time

Someone's gonna HDDT the map.

[player name] 900

Refers to a player getting 900 pp on a single map

SHIGETORA 900

[player name] 1k

Refers to a player getting 1000 pp on a single map

MREKK 1K

Full Combo

Refers to setting a score without breaking your combo (not necessarily ss)

I got a full combo on the map!

OWC

A popular tournament in the community where participating teams (1 each country) can compete for prizes

Are you watching OWC tonight?

Tiebreaker (tb)

The final map of a tournament match, usually the hardest map in the pool

They actually got to tb, it was so exciting!

Scorev1'd

Refers to having higher accuracy and/or combo, and still getting a lower score than your best score on the map

I can't believe I just got scorev1'd!
